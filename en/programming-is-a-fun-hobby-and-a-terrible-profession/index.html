Programming is a fun hobby and a terrible profession

Friday 10th April 2015

Just a few quotes from @substack I find really revelant about my profession.

programming is a fun hobby and a terrible profession

— substack (@substack) April 10, 2015

when you code as a hobby, you can identify a problem worth solving, solve it, then move on

— substack (@substack) April 10, 2015

when you code for money, problems are chosen for you by formal social hierarchies and priorities informed by the bottom line

— substack (@substack) April 10, 2015

and you can't move on without the brinksmanship and overhead of negotiating with someplace new, foregoing sustenance and benefits

— substack (@substack) April 10, 2015
